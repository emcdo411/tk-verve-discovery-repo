Marketing Intelligence Read

TK Klund has built something rare: a high-trust relationship network that already functions as distribution infrastructure. Verve has the science, veteran credibility, athlete access, and product portfolio to scale. The missing layer is not more hustle or more content. The missing layer is the intelligence required to capture, score, and act on content and relationship signals before they disappear into memory, text threads, inboxes, and one-off conversations.

00A · Discovery Questions

Five questions before the first rep.

This is the call entry point. The purpose is to understand whether Verve needs more content, better analysis, sharper positioning, or a real marketing decision system.

When you say content and marketing analysis, are you trying to improve content production volume, understand what is resonating, sharpen positioning, or build a better reporting and decision system around marketing performance?

What marketing decisions are still being made mostly by instinct right now?

Where does the strongest audience or customer signal currently live: website analytics, social engagement, email, ads, sales conversations, customer reviews, ambassador activity, events, or offline relationship notes?

What would make AI genuinely useful to your team: faster content, better insight, cleaner reporting, stronger campaign decisions, better segmentation, or a pre-meeting / pre-campaign brief?

What would you not want AI to touch because the relationship, brand voice, claims, customer data, or trust risk is too high?

01 · Intelligence Gaps

Where the current rep max ends.

Two operating tracks, one core issue: the network and content already produce signal, but no system is turning that signal into prioritized action.

01
Critical · Layer 0B

No Customer Performance Intelligence Loop

Verve sells performance and recovery products to athletes, veterans, and wellness consumers. Testimonials may exist, but there is no visible system capturing outcomes, correlating usage patterns with performance claims, or feeding proof back into messaging and product development.

Customer outcome scoring engine linking product usage patterns to self-reported performance gains.
02
Critical · Layer 0E

Ambassador Network Has No Intelligence Layer

TK's athlete and celebrity relationships are credibility assets. The current gap is AI-assisted engagement tracking, conversion attribution, reach optimization, and renewal signal detection by product line.

Ambassador intelligence dashboard with engagement scoring, attribution, and reach-to-conversion mapping.
03
High · Layer 1

Veteran Segment Is Underintelligenced

Verve's veteran loyalty position has mission value, but it should also become a measurable segment. Purchase patterns, referral behavior, product preferences, and community signal can inform acquisition and formulation priorities.

Veteran segment intelligence brief with referral mapping and mission-aligned product positioning signals.
04
High · Layer 2

Multi-SKU Portfolio Has No Demand Signal Architecture

Mouthguards, CBD and hemp wellness, spray vitamins, and skincare each create different purchase cadences and customer profiles. The portfolio needs demand signals, reorder triggers, and cross-sell intelligence.

SKU-level signal scoring, cross-sell trigger identification, reorder prediction, and seasonal demand modeling.
05
DARE · Layer 0C

No Governance Layer for Relationship-Safe AI

AI recommendations can create damage if they violate brand values or a relationship TK has spent years building. The system needs decision gates that preserve judgment, trust, and context.

Chameleon Governance Model with criteria drift detection and relationship-aware output gates.
06
High · Layer 2

Performance Proof Is Not Packaged as a Sales Asset

The brand should feel like measurable performance, not just wellness. If product claims, athlete proof, and customer outcomes are not packaged into a repeatable evidence layer, the sales story stays dependent on TK telling it live.

Proof-of-performance content engine for pitch decks, ambassador kits, sales briefs, and retail partner conversations.

Endorsement Matching Is Manual and Memory-Dependent

The matching process between athlete, brand, moment, and market depends on TK's judgment. That judgment is valuable, but it needs an AI-assisted scoring surface to scale without losing precision.

Endorsement fit scoring engine with brand-athlete alignment, conflict screening, and deal timing signals.

High · Layer 1

Celebrity Softball Classic Has Untapped Intelligence Value

Event intelligence engine for attendee scoring, sponsor conversion attribution, and year-round pipeline extraction.

High · Layer 2

Personal Brand Architecture Has No Compounding Intelligence Layer

Personal brand intelligence brief with content resonance scoring and speaking opportunity prioritization.

02 · DARE Layer

Data. Agility. Risk. Evolution.

This frames the work like a training block: what is available, what is slow, what can break, and what must evolve to scale.

D

Data

Relationship and content data exists, but it is not systematically captured. The signal lives in conversations, inboxes, texts, events, customer interactions, and social response.

A

Agility

Response speed is person-dependent. If TK sees the signal, action happens. If he does not, opportunity decay begins.

R

Risk

The single-point-of-failure risk is elevated. Both businesses rely heavily on TK's personal recall, trust, and relationship judgment.

E

Evolution

The current architecture was built around TK's capacity. The next architecture should multiply his pattern recognition without replacing his final call.
